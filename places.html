Siddheshwar Temple

Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar (a form of Lord Shiva), this temple is a major landmark near the peaceful Siddheshwar Lake[cite: 14, 15, 16]. It becomes especially crowded during the annual Siddheshwar Yatra festival in January[cite: 17].

Pandharpur

Located about 70 km from Solapur, Pandharpur is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple[cite: 18, 19, 20]. It hosts millions of devotees during the Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi festivals[cite: 21].

Akkalkot

About 40 km from Solapur, Akkalkot is famous for the temple of Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj, known for its calm and peaceful atmosphere[cite: 22, 23, 24, 26].

Solapur Fort (Bhuikot Fort)

Built during the Bahmani Sultanate period, this fort reflects medieval Indian military architecture and historical heritage[cite: 27, 28, 29, 30].
