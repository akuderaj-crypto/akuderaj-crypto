About the City

Solapur is a historic and culturally rich city located in the southern part of Maharashtra, close to the borders of Karnataka and Telangana. The city is known for its spiritual centers, historic monuments, traditional festivals, and vibrant culture.

For centuries, Solapur has been an important destination for pilgrims and travelers. Visitors can experience a blend of spirituality, history, local traditions, and delicious regional cuisine. The city also serves as a gateway to several nearby pilgrimage destinations.

Quick Highlights

  • Famous spiritual places like Siddheshwar Temple, Pandharpur, and Akkalkot.
  • World-renowned traditional textiles like Solapur chaddars and towels.
  • Strategic location well-connected to major Indian metros.
